Quick answers

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.

I have a billing issue or wrong charge.

Email us at hello@aishiksha.in with your account email and the transaction details. We'll sort it out within one business day and refund if applicable.

Can I get a student discount?

Yes — we offer need-based pricing for verified students at Indian colleges. Send us your college email and enrollment proof and we'll set you up.

I found an error in a course / paper.

We really appreciate this — it keeps the content rigorous. Email us or post in Discord's #corrections channel. We'll fix it and credit you in the changelog.

Are you open to partnerships or collaborations?

Yes — for colleges, bootcamps, and companies who want to offer AI Shiksha to their students or teams. Use the form and select "Partnership / collaboration".

I can't access content I've paid for.

Try signing out and back in. If that doesn't work, email us with your account email and we'll restore access manually within a few hours.
