Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes — cancel anytime, no questions asked. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. There are no cancellation fees or lock-in contracts.
Is there a free trial?+
The Foundation Track has 2 lessons free — no signup needed. You can also create a free account and access the glossary, 1 sample paper, and community before subscribing.
What payment methods are accepted?+
UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and net banking. All payments are processed securely via Razorpay.
Can I switch between Learner and Pro?+
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you get Pro access immediately and your billing is prorated. When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Do you offer student or team discounts?+
We're working on team plans. For student discounts, reach out via the contact page — we offer need-based pricing for verified students at Indian colleges.
How often is new content added?+
New courses and papers are added every month. Pro subscribers get early access before the content is released to Learner subscribers. We also host live Q&A sessions monthly for all paid subscribers.
