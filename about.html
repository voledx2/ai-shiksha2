The Problem

Most AI education is surface-level by design

Courses show you how to call an API. They don't show you what happens inside. You learn to use a hammer without understanding metallurgy.

We think that's a problem — especially as AI becomes the foundational layer of all software. The engineers who understand the internals will build the future. The ones who don't will be replaced by it.

Our Values

What we believe

01

Depth over breadth

One well-explained concept is worth more than ten shallow overviews. We go slow, go deep, and don't skip the hard parts.

02

Code is the truth

Theory without implementation is incomplete. Every concept ships with working, annotated code. No hand-waving.

03

Built for India

World-class AI education shouldn't require a foreign visa or a ₹2 lakh course fee. Samajhna chahte ho? Aao.

04

Honest pricing

Two tiers, no tricks. If you can afford ₹99/month, you get the full curriculum. No upsells hiding the good stuff.

05

Research-backed

We don't cite secondary sources. Every claim traces to a paper, a benchmark, or a reproducible experiment.

06

Community first

Learning in isolation is slow. Our Discord isn't an afterthought — it's where some of the best discussions happen.
