Privacy Policy

AI Shiksha ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We believe in plain language — so we've written this to actually be readable.

1. What we collect

We collect information you provide directly, and some information automatically when you use our platform.

Information you give us

  • Name and email address when you create an account or contact us
  • Payment information (processed by Razorpay — we never store raw card details)
  • Profile information like your role, experience level, and learning goals
  • Messages you send us via the contact form or email
  • Content you post in community spaces (Discord, Q&A sessions)

Information collected automatically

  • Pages visited, lessons started and completed, papers read
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • IP address (stored anonymised after 24 hours)
  • Session duration and navigation patterns (to improve course structure)

We do not sell your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes, ever.

2. How we use your information

We use your data only to operate and improve AI Shiksha. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account and subscription
  • To send you course materials, receipts, and product updates you've opted into
  • To personalise your learning dashboard and course recommendations
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To understand how learners use the platform (aggregate, anonymised analytics)
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax records, GST filings)

We will never use your data to train AI models or share it with AI providers without your explicit consent.

3. Who we share your information with

We share data only with the services required to run AI Shiksha, and only the minimum necessary:

  • Razorpay — payment processing. Subject to Razorpay's privacy policy.
  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database hosting. Data stored in Mumbai region (Asia South 1).
  • Resend — transactional emails (welcome, receipt, password reset).
  • Vercel — website hosting. No personal data is processed by Vercel beyond standard server logs.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority in India. We will notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

4. Cookies and tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies required to operate the platform:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in. Expires when you close your browser or after 30 days.
  • Preference cookie — remembers your settings (theme, language). Expires after 1 year.
  • Analytics cookie — anonymised usage data to improve the platform. You can opt out in your account settings.

We do not use advertising cookies, Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tracking, or any third-party marketing trackers.

5. Payments and billing data

All payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment gateway. We never store your full card number, CVV, or net banking credentials on our servers.

We do store:

  • Your subscription plan, amount, and billing date
  • Razorpay's payment ID (a reference number, not payment data)
  • Your billing name and email for GST invoices

GST invoices are generated for all Indian subscribers and are available in your dashboard. We retain payment records for 7 years as required by Indian accounting law.

6. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — update incorrect or incomplete information via your dashboard or by emailing us
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV)
  • Objection — opt out of non-essential communications at any time
To exercise any of these rights, email hello@aishiksha.in with the subject "Data Request". We respond within 7 business days.

Deleting your account will remove your learning progress, profile, and personal data. Payment records required by law (GST) will be retained for the legally required period in anonymised form.

7. Children's privacy

AI Shiksha is intended for users who are 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, for significant changes, send an email notification to all registered users at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Your continued use of AI Shiksha after changes are effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
